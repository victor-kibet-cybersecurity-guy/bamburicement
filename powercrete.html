Using Bamburi Powercrete Cement

Surface Preparation

For optimal Powercrete results, rigorous surface preparation is essential. Ensure a stable, aggressively roughened substrate. Remove all loose material, weak concrete, and contaminants. Eliminate oils and chemicals with heavy-duty scabbling or high-pressure blasting. Clean rebar thoroughly. This preparation guarantees a superior bond, ensuring durable, high-strength Powercrete structures.

  • Remove dust, dirt, and loose particles from the surface.
  • Clear any grease or oil stains completely.
  • Level the surface for uniform application.
  • Lightly moisten the area before use (avoid soaking).
  • Repair cracks or weak spots beforehand.

Choose POWERCRETE 52.5 for ultra-strong, fast-setting concrete that lasts

Mixing

Mixing POWERCRETE 52.5 correctly unlocks its highest strength and efficiency for precast or industrial uses. Use clean water and quality aggregates, measured precisely with a bucket or wheelbarrow. Blend until uniform, minimizing water to maintain strength and reduce paste, ensuring a durable mix that guards against alkali-aggregate reactions.

  • Use clean water free of salts or impurities.
  • Select aggregates meeting quality standards.
  • Measure cement, sand, and aggregates accurately.
  • Stir until the mix is a consistent color.
  • Avoid overmixing to preserve strength.

Application

Applying POWERCRETE 52.5 effectively harnesses its ultra-high strength for slabs, poles, or foundations. Spread evenly, compact thoroughly, and finish smoothly to leverage its fast-setting nature. Follow mix designs and cure well to minimize cracking, ensuring top performance and durability in high-strength concrete for bridges or heavy-duty floors.

  • Spread the mix evenly over the surface.
  • Compact concrete to remove air voids.
  • Finish surfaces smoothly for optimal results.
  • Apply in layers for thick sections if needed.
  • Start curing shortly after initial setting.

Precautions

Safety and care are key with POWERCRETE 52.5 to maintain its ultra-high strength benefits. Wear protective gear to avoid irritation, store cement dry on raised platforms, and use it first-in, first-out. Cure for at least 7 days to boost strength, use minimal water, and handle safely. This ensures top performance in demanding concrete applications.

  • Wear safety glasses, gloves, and a dust mask.
  • Store cement on raised timber or plastic sheets.
  • Keep stacks dry and away from walls.
  • Use cement in order of receipt (FIFO).
  • Cure concrete for at least 7 days.
