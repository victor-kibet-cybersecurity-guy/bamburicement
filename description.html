Bamburi Fundi

Masonry Cement

Bamburi Fundi is a specialized masonry cement designed for plastering, mortar, and block laying. Formulated to enhance workability, it provides smooth finishes and increased durability. This cement is ideal for non-structural applications, offering reliable performance for general construction needs.

Perfect for homeowners, fundis, and small contractors looking for a cost-effective solution.

Interior Plastering
Exterior Rendering
Floor Screeding
Repair Works
Pointing Joints
