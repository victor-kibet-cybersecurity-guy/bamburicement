Using Bamburi Fundi Cement

Surface Preparation

For optimal results with Bamburi Fundi Cement, proper surface preparation is vital. Ensure the substrate is stable and roughened. Remove any loose materials or existing weak plaster. Eliminate contaminants like oil or paint using a wire brush or chisel. This prepares the surface for a strong bond, ensuring durable and lasting masonry work.

  • Clear contaminants that could weaken adhesion

Mixing

Bamburi Fundi Cement is simple for optimal mortar performance. Use 5 per 25kg bag, free of impurities like mud or salts. Blend in a wheelbarrow or bucket, stirring thoroughly until the mix is smooth and uniform in color. Avoid excess water to maintain strength

  • Use Clean Water

Storage & Usage Information

Precaution

The Bamburi Fundi Cement is for production of mortar for laying masonry and mortar for rendering and plastering only.

Handling & Storage

Store bagged masonry cement on timber pallets at least 10cm above the floor in a dry building free from moisture or water.

Water

Use clean water without impurities (e.g salts, grass, mud etc) for mixing plaster of mortar.

Masonry Cement Usage

Poor quality sand and improper mix proportioning reduces the strength of mortar.

Masonry cement can last over 90 days if handled and stored properly.
