Eliminate contaminants like dust or grease using a wire brush or pressure wash. This prepares the surface for a strong bond, ensuring durable and lasting structural performance.

Mixing

Mixing POWERMAX 42.5 properly unlocks its superior workability and strength for projects like bridges or slabs. Use clean water and aggregates meeting standards, measured precisely with a bucket or wheelbarrow.
